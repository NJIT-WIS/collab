Our Story

Welcome to mywebclass.org, an open source software project committed to creating connections between educators and professionals in the software industry by building a community of people committed to helping students of all ages and backgrounds succeed.

Our project was founded in 2023 by Keith Williams, a University Lecturer at New Jersey Institute of Technology with over 30 years of experience as a software engineer, entrepreneur, and 17 years of experience as a university lecturer and in k-12 education. Keith founded mywebclass.org to provide a practical, hands-on learning experience for students and to help teachers stay up-to-date with the latest technologies.

Our open source software project is designed to provide educational opportunities to students and teachers, allowing them to gain experience with new technologies and gain exposure to different areas of software engineering, all while contributing to a real-world project. We believe that mentorship is key to helping students and teachers develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed in the modern software industry.

We use tools like ChatGPT and Dale-2 to help write, code, and make images for mywebclass.org, which provides a platform for students and teachers to gain experience with new technologies and learn advanced software engineering concepts. By using these tools, we can provide a more personalized and effective learning experience for our users.

Our project is run by a team of passionate volunteers who are committed to our cause. We rely on the support of our donors to continue our work, and we are grateful for every contribution we receive. Your donations help us to provide the resources and tools necessary for teachers and students to succeed.

Thank you for your interest in mywebclass.org. Together, we can create a community of people committed to helping students of all ages and backgrounds succeed in the modern software industry.
